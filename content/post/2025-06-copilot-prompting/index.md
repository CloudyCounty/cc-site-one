A hands-on training session focused on getting better results from Copilot through stronger prompting. We tour the refreshed Copilot interface and clarify the difference between the consumer and enterprise experiences.

**The 4-part prompt framework**

1. **Goal** — what you want Copilot to do.
2. **Context** — the background it needs to do it well.
3. **Source** — the data, file, or grounding it should use.
4. **Expectation** — the format, length, and tone of the answer.

We explore the **Prompt Gallery** for inspiration, demonstrate generating a meeting summary from a transcript, and run a live activity where attendees refine a weak prompt step by step into a high-quality result.
