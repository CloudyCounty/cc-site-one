# About the Cloudy County Community Calls

The **Cloudy County Community Calls** are a monthly gathering for anyone working in — or curious about — the Microsoft 365 cloud. Hosted by **The #ShiftShow**, each call covers:

- **What's New & Near** — a rolling look at what just shipped across M365 and what's coming next.
- **A featured topic** — a deep dive on a specific product or scenario (Copilot, Teams, Forms, Viva, SharePoint, and more).
- **Live demos and Q&A** — practical walkthroughs you can take back to your own organization.

Every call is recorded and posted to our [YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvY0gBv2JjRpbwOCO0ZjREWof57ilIFNn). This site mirrors the **10 most recent calls** so you can browse, search, and share them more easily.

## How to use this site

- **Categories** group calls by product area (Copilot, Teams, Outlook, Viva, SharePoint, and the monthly M365 Updates roundup).
- **Tags** zoom in on specific features mentioned during a call.
- **Archives** lists every post in chronological order.

Happy cloud-watching ☁️
