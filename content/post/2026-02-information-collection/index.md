In this Cloudy County Community Call we cover the latest Microsoft 365 enhancements and demonstrate practical ways to collect information across your organization.

**What's New highlights**

- Copilot Chat enhancements for finding people and content.
- Improvements to Teams meeting links and scheduling.
- Addition of Claude / Anthropic models alongside the existing OpenAI options.
- Agent mode rolling out more broadly in Excel.
- New Copilot Teach experience for educators.

**Demo: information-collection tools**

The main demo walks through three information-collection tools and when to use each:

- **Microsoft Forms** for structured surveys and quizzes.
- **Microsoft Lists** for tracking items with rich column types.
- **SharePoint document libraries** with metadata for file-based collection.

We compare when to use each, show how to surface responses in Teams, and demonstrate Copilot helping draft questions and analyze results.
